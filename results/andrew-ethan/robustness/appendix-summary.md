# Robustness Notes

- Conversion subsidy raises final average environmental quality from 0.029 to 0.358 and shifts land use toward organic production (64.75%).
- Results-based subsidy moves the system only modestly relative to market under the current calibration (0.035 final average environmental quality).
- Mean farmer profit before subsidy is 1.619 under conversion and 1.219 under results-based payments.
- These cases should be treated as appendix material. They help show robustness, but they are not the cleanest main story.
